How can employers proactively ensure employee well-being?

Continuous improvement in workplace health and safety is the key aspect of ensuring employee well-being. To identify potential risks and prioritize mitigation efforts employers need intelligence into enterprise-wide data and incidents for insights on high-risk areas for action.

Achieve optimal performance and preventive maintenance goals by increasing plant health and employee safety. Reduce incidents and injuries by gathering feedback and improvement ideas from employees. Drive leading indicators in the right direction and share real-time data with employees. Quickly address activities and corrective actions using next generation alerting and event notifications when incidents do occur.

How can employers increase employee participation in establishing a safety culture?

Provide safety training and equipment to encourage employee engagement in the safety process. Train employees using Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR). Leverage sensor-based smart personal protective equipment (PPE) to detect potentially hazardous situations and prevent workers from entering dangerous situations. Empower employees with risk detection and visibility using computer vision technologies.

Employee Health and Safety for Greater Productivity

In the post-Covid-19 world, workplace health and safety has become even more important at the enterprise level and across the supply chain. Traditional manual methods are no longer enough to track, monitor, and mitigate risks. The need has changed from prevention of illnesses and injuries to ensuring the overall, long-term well-being of employees. Health and safety leaders are looking to bring together the entire organization to make this happen.

The only way to improve workplace safety is by eliminating incidents. The key is to drive continuous improvement of processes and technologies to constantly bring down incident rates. Leverage Next Generation AI and Machine Learning algorithms to ensure employee well-being, greater engagement and improved confidence.

An EHS solution like ComplianceQuest is designed to drive ease of collaboration and engagement among employees that make a huge difference.

How can employers make the workplace incident-free and safe?

Creating awareness about the need to report incidents, whether small or significant, near misses and observations â€” both positive and negative â€” is the first step for any employer to reduce incidents. Providing tools that simplify the reporting process while maintaining anonymity encourage employees to report incidents and work towards zero incidents.

Near misses often go unreported and some incidents may be brushed aside as being too negligible and unworthy of being reported. Employees at times may be hesitant to report incidents fearing retribution, not wanting to appear as tattlers, or because it requires too much effort.

Automate the incident management process and provide employees visibility into the corrective and preventive action taken to rectify and mitigate the risk of occurrence and recurrence. This improves employee confidence in the process and demonstrates your commitment to their health and safety.

Improve Profitability

Health and safety incidents lead to compensations, litigation, and penalties. They cause loss of productivity due to absenteeism. A proactive approach to employee well-being will lead to huge cost savings and accelerate growth.
